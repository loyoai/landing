About MWAN
MWAN is the Kingdom's leading waste management authority, dedicated to transforming Saudi Arabia's approach to waste management through innovative solutions, sustainable practices, and strategic partnerships that align with Vision 2030's environmental goals.
Learn more
Our Vision
Raising awareness about the importance of proper waste management practices, implementing the highest standards to enhance transparency and efficiency in the sector, and contributing to a higher quality of life, all align with the goals of Vision 2030.
Learn more
Our Mission
To lead the transformation of waste management in Saudi Arabia by implementing cutting-edge technologies, fostering circular economy principles, and creating sustainable solutions that protect our environment for future generations.
Learn more
Our Key Tasks
Developing comprehensive waste management strategies, establishing regulatory frameworks, promoting recycling initiatives, and facilitating public-private partnerships to achieve zero waste goals and environmental sustainability across the Kingdom.
